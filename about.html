IMG Integration and Education Trust

Who We Are

For International Medical Graduates (IMGs), arriving in the UK marks the beginning of an exciting yet demanding journey. Beyond adjusting to a new culture, lifestyle, and professional expectations, IMGs must also navigate complex medical licensing processes, workplace dynamics, and career opportunities—all while building a new life in an unfamiliar environment.

IMG Integration & Education Trust is a UK-based charitable organisation focused on supporting International Medical Graduates (IMGs) in their journey to fully integrating into the UK healthcare system. We aim to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity, ensuring that skilled healthcare professionals from diverse backgrounds can flourish and contribute effectively to patient care.

We believe that every qualified doctor deserves an equal chance to succeed, regardless of nationality or socioeconomic background. Through mentorship, education, financial assistance, and advocacy, we help IMGs overcome barriers, build confidence, and establish rewarding careers in the UK.

Our mission and vision

Our mission and vision

Mission

To empower IMGs by providing access to education, professional guidance, and support networks that enable their successful integration into the UK healthcare sector.

Vision

A healthcare system where internationally trained medical professionals are fully included, valued, and equipped with the tools they need to succeed—ultimately improving patient care across the UK.

What We Do

Professional Training & Career Development.

Navigating the UK medical system can be complex for IMGs. That’s why we offer:

Structured training programmes to help IMGs prepare for licensing exams such as PLAB, MRCP, and MRCS.

Career guidance workshops covering CV writing, job applications, and interview preparation.

Specialist mentorship programmes connecting IMGs with experienced UK doctors who provide career advice, clinical skills development, and ongoing support.

Well-being & Mental Health Support.

Adjusting to a new healthcare system can be both emotionally and mentally challenging. We provide:

Peer support networks where IMGs can connect, share experiences, and find encouragement.

Counselling services tailored to address stress, anxiety, and professional burnout.

Work-life balance programmes to help doctors build resilience while maintaining personal and professional well-being.

Financial Aid & Scholarships:

The financial burden of relocating, retraining, and securing necessary qualifications can be overwhelming. To ensure IMGs have a fair opportunity, we offer:

Grants and scholarships to assist with exam fees, relocation costs, and professional development courses.

Subsidised training materials to reduce financial strain on aspiring doctors.

Emergency financial assistance for IMGs facing unexpected hardship.

Advocacy for Equality & Inclusion:

We believe in a healthcare system that values diversity. Our advocacy efforts focus on:

Challenging discrimination and bias against IMGs in the workplace and recruitment processes.

Promoting equal opportunities through policy engagement and partnerships with healthcare institutions.

Educating UK employers on the benefits of hiring and supporting internationally trained doctors.

Public Health & Community Outreach:

Beyond supporting individual IMGs, we are committed to improving healthcare access for marginalised communities. We engage in:

Health awareness campaigns in migrant communities to promote preventive healthcare.

Medical volunteering programmes where IMGs can give back to underserved populations.

Collaboration with NHS trusts and healthcare providers to enhance diversity and inclusion in medical practice.
